About Bon Appetit Haven

We Are A Hotel And Restaurant Where Relaxation Meets Fine Dining.

Bon Appétit Haven is a stylish hotel and restaurant where French elegance meets Ghanaian warmth. We offer cozy rooms, delicious meals, and a welcoming atmosphere — perfect for both restful stays and unforgettable dining experiences.

Experience a delightful fusion of fine French cuisine and rich Ghanaian hospitality. At Bon Appétit, we bring you the taste of Paris with a local twist — crafted with passion, served with love.
